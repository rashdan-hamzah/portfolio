OrderSaap — order automation on WhatsApp

SME ordering & operations

Orders arrived through WhatsApp, calls, and DMs. Owners handle all messaging, fulfilment, & order packing.

  • Some orders are still pending
  • Missed sales from unreplied messages
  • Owners overworked unnecessarily

The issue was operational visibility and no automation.

Mapped the order flows before start coding.

  • Order intake → payment processing → fulfilment → delivery
  • Standardize each step
  • Designed automated WhatsApp order system
  • Centralized orders fulfilment
  • Reduce lead time from 1 day to 2 minutes
  • Clear accountability per order
